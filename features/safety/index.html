Safety & Privacy

2 min read
February 26, 2026

Your Family’s Safety is Our Top Priority

At Shabash, we believe that a tool for families must be built on a foundation of trust. Because we are parents ourselves, we’ve designed Shabash with the same level of security and privacy we demand for our own children.

Privacy by Design

Unlike many other apps, Shabash is not built on data collection. We have a very simple philosophy: Your data belongs to you.

  • Zero Third-Party Ads: We will never clutter our app with ads or expose your children to commercial tracking.
  • No Data Selling: We have never and will never sell your family’s personal information to advertisers or data brokers.
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect the information necessary to provide the service.

Security Measures

We use industry-standard security to ensure your data stays where it belongs.

  • Encrypted Connections: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is protected by SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Secure Authentication: We use modern, secure login methods to prevent unauthorized access to your account.
  • Regular Audits: We constantly review our code and infrastructure to protect against new threats.

Developed for Families

Shabash is focused on creates a positive, safe environment for kids to learn.

  • Protected Profiles: Kids use simplified, protected profiles within the family account.
  • Parental Controls: Parents have full control over all chores, rewards, and star balances.
  • Safe Feedback: We listen to our users to ensure the app remains a healthy part of family life.

“As a developer and a dad, I wouldn’t let my kids use an app I didn’t trust 100%. That’s the bar we set for Shabash every single day.”
