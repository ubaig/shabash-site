## Stay in Sync

Shabash isn't just about chores—it helps the whole family coordinate. The calendar aggregates tasks and custom events, giving everyone a clear view of what’s coming up.

### Features at a Glance

- **Integrated with chores and rewards** for full context.
- **Custom events** can be created by any family member.
- **Notifications** sent when events are added or tasks are completed.
- **Timezone awareness** ensures reminders arrive at the right local time.

Set up recurring family meetings, school projects, or simply remind kids when it’s time to take out the trash—all from the same app.
