Chores

Family Chore Tracking

Create chores, assign them to kids or parents, and track completion with an intuitive shared dashboard.

Recurring Schedules

Define chores that repeat daily, weekly, monthly or custom intervals so nothing is forgotten.

Approval Workflow

Parents can require kids to submit chores for review, ensuring tasks are done correctly before stars are awarded.

Custom Buckets

Organize chores into categories like Morning, Homework, or Yard for easy filtering and focus.

Star Values

Assign a star value to each chore so kids know exactly what they will earn.

Keep Everyone on Task

Shabash makes chore management collaborative. Parents and children can view the same list of tasks and check them off as they are completed. With clear schedules and flexible assignment options, daily routines become easier to manage.

Key Highlights

  • Assign to any family member – choose who should be responsible for each chore.
  • Editable by both parents – families can add or modify chores together.
  • Mark as complete with one tap from the dashboard.

How It Works

  1. Parents or kids both can creates a new chore with title, description, emoji, and star value.
  2. Select a frequency and who it’s assigned to.
  3. Child sees the chore on their dashboard and marks it done when finished.
  4. Parents approve and stars are added to the kid’s balance.

Chores can be paused or archived when they are no longer needed, keeping the list clean and focused.
